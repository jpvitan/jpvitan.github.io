Featured Blog

Probabilistic Primality Testing for Large Numbers

Primality testing of large numbers plays a significant role in public-key cryptosystems like RSA. In fact, the security of RSA relies on two or more large and distinct prime numbers that are often hundreds of digits long. If we cannot guarantee within an acceptable probability that the numbers used for RSA are prime numbers, then the whole cryptosystem would fail.
