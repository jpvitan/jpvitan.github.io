Probabilistic Primality Testing for Large Numbers

Primality testing of large numbers plays a significant role in public-key cryptosystems like RSA. In fact, the security of RSA relies on two or more large and distinct prime numbers that are often hundreds of digits long. If we cannot guarantee the primality of numbers used in RSA, the whole cryptosystem might not function properly or work as expected.
